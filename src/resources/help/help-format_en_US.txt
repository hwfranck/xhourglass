Enter the duration as numbers separated by the colon (<em>:</em>)<br>
or any of the letters <em>h</em>, <em>m</em> or <em>s</em>. These separators help in<br>
the interpretation of each number as <em>H</em>ours, <em>M</em>inutes<br>
or <em>S</em>econds.<br>
Some examples are listed below.<br>

<table>
	<tr>
		<td>16:45:15</td>
		<td rowspan="2">--></td>
		<td rowspan="2">16 hours 45 minutes 15 seconds</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td>16h45m15</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td>45:15</td>
		<td>--></td>
		<td>45 minutes 15 seconds</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td>16h45 = 16h45m</td>
		<td>--></td>
		<td>16 hours 45 minutes</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td>16h36s</td>
		<td>--></td>
		<td>16 hours 36 seconds</td>
	</tr>
	<tr>
		<td>45m = 45</td>
		<td>--></td>
		<td>45 minutes</td>
	</tr>
</table>
